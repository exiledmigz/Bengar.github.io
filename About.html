ABOUT US


BENGAR Industrial Corporation was established in 1990 and is now one of the leading companies that provide products and services vital in the manufacturing processes of various kinds of industries. With over 180 employees, and two major branches, BENGAR meets the individual needs of its clients by providing the right products and services at a fair price.

As one of the country's top 10,000 companies, BENGAR specializes in providing conveyor belts, conveyor systems and engineering plastic parts. It has also branched into the supply and maintenance of diesel power generators. Furthermore, BENGAR has been the official distributor of Hitachi Ink Jet Printer and Hitachi Air Compressor since year 2000. With its dedicated staff, BENGAR has excelled in providing quality products to different kinds of industries mainly the food and beverages, canning, packaging, meat/poultry, mining cement, tobacco, electronic and logistic industry.
